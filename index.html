Experienced Pediatrician

I provide quality and compassionate medical care for children and adolescents from birth to 21 years of age.

Your pediatrician will become a trusted family advisor who guides your parenting decisions when it comes to your newborn's health care.

Main responsibilities for pediatrician:

  • Assessing children's growth and development, including cognitive skills.

  • Prescribing medications and treatments.

  • Advising parents about health and diet issues impacting their children.

Choosing a pediatrician

Choosing a pediatrician is an important part of preparing for your new baby. Finding a pediatrician is not hard, but you need to begin as soon as possible. You can ask your obstetrician for names, and talk with other parents about their pediatrician. It is often a good idea to meet with two or three prospective pediatricians before your baby is born.
